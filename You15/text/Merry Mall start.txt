Merry Mall start

To launch your multi-stream network with the highest chance of fast growth and seamless technical execution, Merry Mall is the absolute best channel to start first.
Starting with your cozy, relaxing stream offers three major advantages that will help you build your platform safely before introducing your more chaotic concepts:
1. The YouTube Algorithm Favors Comfort First
The "lo-fi study beats" and "cozy bedroom ambient" search categories have massive, predictable traffic volume 24/7.

	•	Students, software developers, and remote workers search for these keywords daily and keep streams open for hours to help them focus.
	•	Starting with Merry Mall allows you to tap into an established audience instantly, giving your channel its first vital wave of continuous watch-time hours, which tricks the algorithm into pushing your stream to the main page.
2. High Advertiser Demand for Launch Cash
It is significantly easier to onboard your first real-world advertisers for a clean, relaxing, aesthetic stream than a scary or chaotic one.

	•	Brands (especially indie clothing lines, sticker shops, or stationery companies) love the "cozy study vibe" because it feels safe, premium, and aligns perfectly with an audience that has purchasing power.
	•	Securing early sponsorship revenue on Merry Mall gives you immediate funding to pay your background artist for the custom assets you'll need for Mystery Mall and Madness Mall.
3. Your Programming "Safety Sandbox"
From a technical standpoint, Merry Mall uses your cleanest, most predictable mathematical code.

	•	The chord progressions stay locked in pleasant Major 7th scales, the graphics use standard daytime crossfades, and the security guard follows a smooth, rhythmic patrol path.
	•	This makes it the perfect "sandbox" to test your automated Windows direct-to-RAM scripts, verify your hardware temperatures over a 72-hour run, and calibrate your Discord webhook error-recovery loops. If your code handles a basic, stable stream perfectly for a week, you can confidently copy-paste that engine and unleash the chaotic code modifiers needed for your other channels.
