1. The Scaling Split Mathematics
The revenue share pool is divided into three buckets, totaling a maximum of 100% payout. Your share is fixed, while the content buckets scale dynamically between 0% and 33.333% based on actual utilization over your total loop cycle (e.g., 24 hours / 2,592,000 frames total).
The algebraic equations for your billing cycle splits look like this:
$$\text{Owner Share} = 33.333\%$$ 
$$\text{Art Share} = \left( \frac{\text{Frames of Contributed Human Art}}{\text{Total Day Cycle Frames}} \right) \times 33.333\%$$ 
$$\text{Music Share} = \left( \frac{\text{Seconds of Contributed Human Music}}{\text{Total Day Cycle Seconds}} \right) \times 33.333\%$$ 
$$\text{Platform Overhead / Reinvestment} = 100\% - (\text{Owner} + \text{Art} + \text{Music})$$ 

	•	The Financial Cushion: If a specific 6-hour block uses 100% AI art and 100% AI music, the Art and Music shares drop to 0%. The remaining 66.666% stays with the channel, acting as platform profit or a buffer fund to invest back into marketing or infrastructure.

3. Automated Revenue Allocation Module
Add this accounting module directly into your project directory. It parses your tracklist array and asset configurations, reads your ad platform monthly payout report, and outputs an audit sheet detailing exactly how much cash to transfer to each creative contributor.
Save this script as calculate_payouts.py:
import csv

def generate_monthly_payout_audit(monthly_ad_revenue):
    # Total operational constants for a full 24-hour stream cycle
    TOTAL_DAY_SECONDS = 24 * 60 * 60 # 86,400 seconds
    
    owner_guaranteed_share = 0.33333 * monthly_ad_revenue
    total_art_payout = 0
    total_music_payout = 0
    
    contributors = []
    
    print(f"=== PROCESSING DISTRIBUTION AUDIT FOR: ${monthly_ad_revenue:,.2f} ===")
    
    # 1. READ RAW REVENUE LEDGER
    try:
        with open("contributor_ledger.csv", mode="r") as file:
            reader = csv.DictReader(file)
            for row in reader:
                contributors.append(row)
    except FileNotFoundError:
        print("[ERROR] 'contributor_ledger.csv' tracking matrix missing!")
        return

    # 2. RUN MATRIX SPLIT CALCULATIONS
    print("\n--- INDIVIDUAL PAYOUT MANIFEST ---")
    for person in contributors:
        name = person["Contributor_Name"]
        asset_type = person["Asset_Type"]
        
        if asset_type == "music":
            # Scale music: (Track Duration / 24 Hours) * 33.333% of total revenue
            duration = float(person["Metric_Weight"].split()[0])
            coverage_pct = duration / TOTAL_DAY_SECONDS
            individual_payout = (coverage_pct * 0.33333) * monthly_ad_revenue
            total_music_payout += individual_payout
            
        elif asset_type == "art":
            # Scale art: (Hours Active / 24 Hours) * 33.333% of total revenue
            hours_active = float(person["Metric_Weight"].split()[0])
            coverage_pct = hours_active / 24.0
            individual_payout = (coverage_pct * 0.33333) * monthly_ad_revenue
            total_art_payout += individual_payout
            
        else:
            individual_payout = 0.0
            
        if individual_payout > 0:
            print(f"������ {name} ({asset_type.upper()}): Earnings Split: ${individual_payout:.2f}")

    # 3. CONSOLIDATED PLATFORM SUMMARY REPORT
    platform_retention = monthly_ad_revenue - (owner_guaranteed_share + total_art_payout + total_music_payout)
    
    print("\n================ FINANCIAL SHARE SUMMARY ================")
    print(f"Guaranteed Owner Share (33.3%):   ${owner_guaranteed_share:,.2f}")
    print(f"Total Scaled Human Art Payout:     ${total_art_payout:,.2f}")
    print(f"Total Scaled Human Music Payout:   ${total_music_payout:,.2f}")
    print(f"System Surplus Retained by Platform: ${platform_retention:,.2f}")
    print("=========================================================")

# Test calculation using a mock monthly AdSense revenue of $5,000
